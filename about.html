Who We Are

Over the decades, BCCI has evolved into a comprehensive support system for the business ecosystem of Bidar. We organize workshops, trade exhibitions, business meets, and policy dialogues to equip our members with the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in a competitive environment. We actively engage with local, state, and central government bodies to ensure that the voice of Bidar's business community is heard and their concerns addressed.

Our Mission

To accelerate the economic and industrial growth of Bidar by providing a platform for collaboration, knowledge sharing, and policy advocacy. We strive to create a business-friendly environment that attracts investment, fosters entrepreneurship, and generates employment opportunities for the people of Bidar.

Our Vision

To position Bidar as a dynamic hub for trade, industry, and innovation in North Karnataka. We envision a future where every entrepreneur in Bidar has access to world-class resources, mentorship, and government support to realize their business ambitions and contribute to the district's prosperity.
